Nudibranchs, or sea slugs, are beautiful marine creatures, but they are not familiar to most. The objective of this project is to design nudibranch-inspired themes for sample layouts and elements of websites. However, the objective here is not to spread awareness or inform people about them.

But why haven't I heard about nudibranchs before?

Nudibranchs are notoriously incapable of being kept in captivity. The main reason is that each species of nudibranchs is highly reliant on a singular prey. Unlike other marine life that can adapt to frozen fish or shrimp, flakes, and pellets, nudibranchs will instead starve to death, so to sustain them in captivity means acquiring the prey specific to a species, presuming it's known and consistently available.

If you'd like to know more: seaslug.world
